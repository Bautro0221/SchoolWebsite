Bachelor of Fine Arts Major in Painting in the Philippines



Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) Major in Painting is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that is concerned with producing artistic representations through the use of paint. This program focuses on developing the student’s perceptual and technical skills in painting.

Taking this major will help you learn about the basics of drawing, the different tools used in painting, the types of surfaces paintings are usually done in, and the use of visual perception in introducing different concepts in your paintings.

Some of the courses that you may take while enrolled in this program include:

  • Visual Perception
  • Visual Studies
  • Visual Communication
  • Techniques
  • Drawing
  • Painting
  • Materials
  • Art Theory
  • Art History
  • Art Workshop
  • Research Method in the Arts

Aside from the courses mentioned above, you may also be given the opportunity to work at art museums, advertising agencies, printing presses, or other similar companies that can hone your talents and skills as an aspiring painter as part of your On-the-Job Training (OJT).

CAREER

Graduates of BFA Major in Painting can be employed in any of the following positions:

  • Painter
  • Illustrator
  • Production Designer
  • Visual Communicators
  • Costume Designer
  • Graphic Artist
  • Professor / Art Educators
  • Art Critics
  • Art Historians
  • Fine Art Appraiser
  • Or other similar art-related positions
