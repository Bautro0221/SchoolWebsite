Specialized Subjects in the ABM Strand

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand equips students with essential knowledge and skills for business-related courses and careers. Here’s a closer look at the core subjects offered:

List of Subjects

Subject Description
Applied Economics Learn to apply economic principles in analyzing industries and spotting business opportunities.
Business Ethics and Social Responsibility Understand ethical practices in business and the importance of accountability and responsibility.
Fundamentals of Accountancy, Business, and Management 1 Explore the basics of accounting, including analyzing business transactions and preparing financial statements.
Business Math Master essential math skills used in business, such as percentages, ratios, and financial analysis.
Principles of Marketing Dive into the principles of marketing and strategies for promoting goods and services effectively.
Work Immersion Gain hands-on experience by simulating real-world business processes, from planning to execution.
