Bachelor in Landscape Architecture in the Philippines



Bachelor in Landscape Architecture (BLA) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that is mainly concerned with the design and planning of land areas. It is closely related with Bachelor of Science in Architecture

, although BS Architecture is primarily concerned with the construction of buildings and infrastructures while Bachelor of Landscape Architecture is more focused on the planning and development of the land that surrounds a particular structure.

By enrolling in this program, you will not only learn about the visual aspects of architectural design, you will also gain a deeper understanding of the factors that need to be considered when designing and developing land areas such as accessibility, functionality, amenability to future design changes, and overall safety among other things.

Some examples of courses that you may take through the course of your study include:

  • Architectural Research
  • Architectural Communication
  • Planning and Urban Design
  • Landscape Ecology
  • Landscape Lighting
  • Hardscape Construction
  • Site Grading and Landform Design
  • Utilities for Sites and Landscape
  • Tropical Landscape Maintenance and Management
  • Regional Landscape Technology
  • Professional Practice and Office Administration
  • Planting Design
  • Theory of Landscape Architecture Design
  • History of Landscape Architecture
  • Landscape Design
