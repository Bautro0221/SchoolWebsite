Bachelor of Arts in Economics in the Philippines



The Bachelor of Arts in Economics (AB Economics) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that will help you gain a better understanding of economic systems and their structure. This includes the entities that compose them, their relationship with one another, and how the introduction of external factors can affect not only them but the entire system as well.

It touches on topics such as economic production, goods and service consumption, wealth distribution, labor, power relations, and taxation among other things.

CAREER

Qualified graduates of AB Economics can find employment as any of the following:

  • Auditor
  • Economics Instructor
  • Economic Development Officer
  • Government Policy Analyst
  • Investment Banker
  • Financial Analyst
  • Researcher
  • Stock Broker

  • Historical Consultant
  • Legal Aide
  • Diplomatic Service
  • Professor
