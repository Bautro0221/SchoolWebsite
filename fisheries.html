Bachelor of Science in Fisheries in the Philippines



The Bachelor of Science in Fisheries (BSFi) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that will train you in aquaculture, capture fisheries, post-harvest fisheries, aquatic ecology, and fisheries-related research.

Some examples of courses that you may take while enrolled in this program include:

  • Project Development and Management
  • Fisheries Entrepreneurship
  • Fisheries Extension
  • Aquaculture
  • Aquatic Ecology
  • Aquatic Resources
  • Ichthyology
  • Fisheries Management
  • Oceanography
  • Philippine Fishing Ground
  • Capture Fisheries
  • Post-harvest Fisheries
  • Meteorology
  • Fisheries Laws
  • Computer Use in Fisheries
  • Research Design and Methodologies
  • Fishery Product Analysis (Elective)
  • Fish Genetics and Breeding (Elective)
  • Mariculture (Elective)
  • Fish Health Management (Elective)
  • Freshwater Aquaculture (Elective)
  • Hatchery Management (Elective)
  • Fishing Systems (Elective)
  • Food Engineering (Elective)
  • Fish Processing Plant Management (Elective)
  • Minor Fishery Products and By-product Processing (Elective)

In addition to taking the subjects mentioned above, you may also undergo an optional On-the-Job Training at any government agencies, private companies, or groups or organizations involved in the practice of fishery.

CAREERS

  • Fisheries Management
  • Fisheries Research
  • Fisheries Extension Service
  • Fisheries Industries
  • Fisheries Instruction
