Bachelor of Science in Biology in the Philippines



The Bachelor of Science in Biology (BS Biology)

is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that deals with the study of living things.

If you have plans of applying to a medical school in order to be a doctor, this is one of the programs that you can use as a stepping stone. Although this is a known pre-med undergraduate program, there are also other career opportunities that you can find in universities, hospitals, laboratories etc as a graduate of BS Biology.

Some of the things that you may learn as a Biology student are the forms and structures of living things; their general characteristics; their origins; the places they live in; and their relationship with man, their environment, and with one another.

Most of the courses you will take in BS Biology have laboratory units. Some of them may include:

  • General Biology
  • Morphoanatomy (Plant)
  • Morphoanatomy (Animal)
  • Physiology (Plant)
  • Physiology (Animal)
  • Systematic Biology (Plant or Animal)
  • Developmental Biology (Plant or Animal)
  • Genetics
  • Ecology
  • Microbiology
  • Cell and Molecular Biology
  • General and Inorganic Chemistry
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Biochemistry
  • General Physics (Mechanics, Electricity and Magnetism)
  • Modern Physics
  • Mathematics
  • Trigonometry
  • Calculus
  • Analytical Geometry
  • Statistics
  • Entomology (Elective)
  • Histology (Elective)
  • Immunology (Elective)
  • Theoretical Biology (Elective)
  • Evolution (Elective)
  • Freshwater Biology (Elective)
  • Marine Biology (Elective)
  • Molecular Biology and Biotechnology (Elective)
  • Genetics (Elective)
  • Systematics (Elective)
  • Microbiology (Elective)
