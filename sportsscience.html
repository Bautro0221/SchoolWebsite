Bachelor of Sports Science in the Philippines



Bachelor of Sports Science (BSS) is a four-year degree program that will train you in the application of scientific principles to sporting or other similar physical activities to ensure that optimum performance will be delivered while eliminating or reducing the risk of acquiring injuries in the process.

Some examples of courses that you may take while enrolled in this program include:

  • Intro to Physical Education & Sport Sciences
  • Sports Science
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Tests & Measurements in Human Movements
  • Exercise Physiology
  • Acquisition of Motor Skills
  • Sports in Society & Culture
  • Biomechanics
  • Psychology in Sports
  • Methods of Physical Conditioning
  • Prevention & Management of Human Movement Related Injuries
  • Ergogenics in Human Movement Related Activities
  • Principles of Coaching
  • Management of Sports & Recreation Related Services
  • Psychology of Sports

CAREERS

Qualified graduates of Bachelor of Sports Science may find employment as any of the following:

  • Physical Education Teacher
  • Sports Coach
  • Fitness Trainer
  • Umpire / Referee
  • Sports Facility Administrator
  • Researcher
