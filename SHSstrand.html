List of Senior High School (SHS) Tracks and Strands

Academic Track

The Academic Track offers four different strands for students to choose from, depending on their interests and future plans.

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) Strand

The ABM Strand is ideal for students who want to pursue business-related courses or careers in business, entrepreneurship, and management.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Strand

The STEM Strand is perfect for students who have an interest in Math, Science, and Engineering, and want to continue in these fields at the university level.

Humanities and Social Science (HUMSS) Strand

The HUMSS Strand is designed for students who want to pursue courses in the fields of Humanities and Social Sciences, such as literature, history, and psychology.

General Academic Strand (GAS)

The GAS is for students who are still unsure about which specific strand to choose. It provides flexibility by offering a combination of subjects from different tracks, allowing students to explore various fields before deciding on a specialization.

Arts and Design Track

The Arts and Design Track covers a variety of artistic fields, including Theater, Music, Dance, Creative Writing, Visual Arts, and Media Arts. Prior to enrollment, students undergo an art/creative talent assessment to determine their artistic inclination and aptitude. This track offers six common subjects that help students develop the necessary skills to specialize in different art forms.

Sports Track

The Sports Track is intended for students interested in sports-related careers, such as athlete development, fitness training, coaching, and more. This track provides a comprehensive study of the various aspects of sports and physical education.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

The TVL Track offers specializations in four major areas: Agri-Fishery Arts, Home Economics, Information and Communications Technology (ICT), and Industrial Arts. This track equips students with practical skills in various technical and vocational fields, preparing them for employment or entrepreneurship.

TVL Track: Agricultural-Fishery Arts (AFA) Strand

The AFA Strand focuses on developing skills related to the cultivation of plants, animal production, and fishery management. Students gain hands-on experience in farming and aquaculture, learning to use modern technologies for agricultural and fishery practices.

TVL Track: Home Economics (HE) Strand

The HE Strand covers areas like garments, tourism, health, processed food, beverages, and community service. Students are trained in practical skills for personal and family care, as well as in providing services to the broader community.

TVL Track: Industrial Arts (IA) Strand

The IA Strand equips students with skills in automotive, construction, electronics, furniture, metalworking, and other industrial arts. It focuses on practical knowledge and skills in repair, maintenance, and the application of industrial technologies.

TVL Track: Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Strand

The ICT Strand covers areas in computer science, system design, and customer service. Students are trained to manage technology, design systems, and apply tech-based solutions in various industries.
