Senior High School (SHS) Core Curriculum Subjects

Oral Communication

Develop listening and speaking skills for effective communication.

Reading and Writing

Develop skills for reading and writing beyond fiction and drama.

Komunikasyon at Pananaliksik

Study the communication and research aspects of Filipino culture.

Pagbasa at Pagsusuri

Learn the process of reading and analyzing texts for research.

21st-century Literature

Appreciate and critically study 21st-century literature.

Contemporary Philippine Arts

Explore various contemporary art practices and styles.

Media and Information Literacy

Understand media and information as tools for communication and development.

General Mathematics

Learn the principles of math and their real-life applications.

Statistics and Probability

Explore statistical analysis and probability theory in real-life problems.

Earth and Life Science

Learn about Earth science and biology through geologic time and life processes.

Physical Science

Study the evolution of matter, motion, and energy in science.

Personal Development

Explore personal growth and self-awareness in life and career decisions.

Understanding Culture, Politics, and Society

Study social, political, and cultural dynamics in the Philippines.

Introduction to Philosophy of the Human Person

Explore philosophical concepts like freedom, existence, and society.

Physical Education and Health

Learn various activities such as fitness exercises, sports, and recreational activities.
