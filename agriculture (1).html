Bachelor of Science in Agriculture in the Philippines



The Bachelor of Science in Agriculture (BSA) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that is concerned with applying the principles of science, ethics, and business management in the production, development, and processing of plants and animals used for food, recreation, industrial, and medical purposes among others.

Some of the courses / subjects that are usually included in the curriculum are:

  • Animal Science
  • Soil Science
  • Crop Science
  • Post Harvest Handling and Seed Technology
  • Agricultural Economics and Marketing
  • Soil Survey, Classification, and Land Use
  • General Physiology and Toxicology
  • Methods of Agricultural Research
  • Fundamentals of Agricultural Engineering
  • Principles and Practices of Plant Breeding, Propagation, and Nursery Management
  • Agricultural Policy and Development
  • Biotechnology and Society
